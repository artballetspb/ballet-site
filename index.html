About Elena Vaganova Technique

If you’ve opened this page, it means you want to learn more about the ballet class, gain knowledge, develop your abilities, discover the secrets of Vaganova, and explore the Russian style of ballet training and the richness that the art of ballet offers.

You’ve found a place where stable, steadily progressing technique is built — where knowledge is shared, potential is developed, and ballet is approached as true art and creative expression. These classes not only support personal growth, but also help students prepare for professional ballet schools, competitions, and auditions. We also offer teacher masterclasses and share the deeper principles of the Vaganova system. Classes are available for children, adult enthusiasts of all levels, and ballet instructors.

Online

In 2020, the entire world of education faced a major challenge — and ballet was no exception. The familiar format of in-studio classes suddenly had a competitor: the online class. Now, with extensive experience teaching online, I can confidently say — it works, and it delivers results. Today, correcting mistakes must be extremely precise, and the ability to see an error must be nearly flawless. I’m grateful for the circumstances that pushed us to grow and take real professional steps forward.
